To compensate for the loss of critically endangered Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain from the site of the approved action, the person taking the action must establish a conservation offset at the Warrayure Site. In establishing the conservation offset the person taking the action must undertake the following:

Within 1 month after the date of this approval enter into an agreement to secure the Warrayure Site in perpetuity under section 173 of the Victorian Planning and Environment Act 1987 or section 3A of the Victorian Conservation Trust Act 1972.
Within 1 month after the date of this approval ensure that the agreement described in condition 3(a) is executed and is registered on the relevant title documents. The Department must be provided with a copy of the signed agreement; and
