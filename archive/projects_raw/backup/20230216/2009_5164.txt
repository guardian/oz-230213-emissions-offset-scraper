The person taking the action must submit a Biodiversity Offset Strategy for the Box Gum Woodland and Derived Grasslands and Swift Parrot, Superb Parrot and Regent Honeyeater to the Minister for approval. The strategy must address the following requirements:

The acquisition and conservation of land containing a minimum of 10ha of vegetation identified as the EPBC Act listed endangered ecological community Box Gum Woodland and Derived Grasslands for every hectare removed, and the acquisition and conservation of five hectares for every hectare exposed to edge effects. The vegetation must be equal or greater quality to that being removed for the Hume Highway Upgrade Holbrook Bypass;
The acquisition and conservation of land containing a minimum of 10 ha of NSW listed White Box Yellow Box Blakely's Red Gum Woodland and 10ha of River Red Gum woodland community number 79 (Benson 2008), for every hectare removed;
The land referred to in 2(a) and 2(b) must be suitable breeding, foraging and nesting habitat for the Swift Parrot, Superb Parrot and Regent Honeyeater to be confirmed by an independent ecological expert;
The land referred to in condition 2(a) and 2(b) must be located within 100 km of the proposed Hume Highway Upgrade Holbrook Bypass;
The land referred to in condition 2(a) and 2(b) must provide some linkage between existing conservation reserves, and/or be adjacent to existing conservation reserves;
The land referred to in condition 2(a) and 2(b) must be protected by a legal instrument under relevant nature conservation legislation, to ensure that the land is conserved in perpetuity;
The strategy must include measures that ensure the land referred to in condition 2(a) and 2(b) will be managed in perpetuity;
Timeframes for the completion of all actions outlined in the strategy, including the acquisition of land to be used as the offset; and
The strategy must be developed in consultation with the Department.

The strategy must be submitted for approval by the Minister within six months of the date of this approval.
The submitted strategy must satisfy the Minister.
