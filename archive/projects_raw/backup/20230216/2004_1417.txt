The Conservation Lands CMP must apply to the Conservation Lands identified in the map at Schedule 1 and the Deferred Areas identified in the map at Schedule 5. The Conservation Lands CMP must set out objectives and specific, measurable, achievable and time-bound actions for the long term management of relevant EPBC Act listed species and associated habitat and vegetation, including the Swift Parrot (Lathamus discolor), Regent Honeyeater (Xanthomyza phrygia), Grey-headed Flying-fox (Pteropus poliocephalus), Bynoes Wattle (Acacia bynoeana), Slaty Red Gum (Eucalyptus glaucina) Grevillea parviflora subsp. Parviflora and Rutidosis Heterogama.
Without limiting the scope of these requirements, the Conservation Lands CMP should include provisions which relate to:

the development of objectives that will ensure the ongoing protection of EPBC listed species and their associated habitat and vegetation;
actions to identify and ensure the retention for conservation purposes of important populations, associated habitat, vegetation buffers and corridors on each site;
actions to manage for conservation purposes retained populations, associated habitat, vegetation buffers and corridors on each site;
regular scheduled reviews of the effectiveness of the management arrangements;
arrangements for the design and implementation of appropriate adaptive management responses and corrective actions should the reviews indicate these are required; and
regular reporting arrangements.
