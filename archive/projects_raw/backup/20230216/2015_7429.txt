The approval holder must prepare and submit an Offset Plan (Plan) for the approval of the Minister to offset the loss of 12.6 ha of black cockatoo habitat. The approval holder must not commence clearing unless the Minister has approved the Plan.
The Plan must include but is not limited to, the following.
Revegetation


Revegetation of 4.9 ha of black cockatoo habitat within the Revegetation area as indicated at Attachment A.


Objectives, targets and completion criteria for the revegetation, including site preparation works, seed broadcast and seedling planting program, success rates and details of replanting requirements if success rates are not achieved.


Management measures including fencing and access, control of fire, weeds, Phytophthora dieback, and erosion.


Conservation arrangements for the Revegetation area.


Timeframes and implementation for the above measures.


Descriptions of the roles and responsibilities of personnel associated with implementation of each of the above measures.


Offset Site (s)


Objectives and goals.


The size (ha) and location, including diagrams, of the offset site(s) required to offset the loss of 12.6 ha of black cockatoo habitat and discussion of how this is consistent with the Environmental Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act 1999 Environmental Offsets Policy (October 2012).


A completed Offsets Assessment Guide with a table justifying the figures used. The Offsets Assessments Guide must be approved by the Department, prior to the submission of the Plan for approval by the Minister.


Conservation arrangements to ensure the offset site(s) are permanently protected, including timeframes for implementation.


Within 12 months of the commencement of the action (or otherwise agreed by the Department in writing), the approval holder must provide the Department with offset attributes and a shapefile.
