The person taking the action must provide offsets for the Fitzroy River turtle (Rheodytes leukops), Squatter pigeon (Southern) (Geophaps scripta scripta) and Brigalow (Acacia Harpophytta) (dominant and co-dominant) ecological community in accordance with the "Offset Plan" dated 16 April 2012;
The following direct offsets must be legally secured prior to commencement of construction:

offsets for Squatter pigeon (Southern) (Geophaps scripta scripta) pigeon; and
land based offsets for Fitzroy River turtle (Rheodytes leukops) upstream of the dam as described in the Offset Plan.


The following direct offsets must be legally secured within 12 months of commencement of construction or such further period as the Minister allows in writing:

land based offsets for Fitzroy River turtle (Rheodytes leukops) downstream of the dam; which may be identified and addressed in the Fitzroy River Turtle Conservation Plan referred to in condition 9 of this approval.
offsets for Brigalow (Acacia harpophylla) (dominant and co-dominant) ecological community.


Where clearing permitted under conditions 12(a) and 13(a) is more than what is permitted under conditions 12 and 13, the person taking the action must amend the Offset Plan to re-calculate the required offsets to allow for any increase in permitted clearing within 20 business days of the increased clearing becoming known. The amended Offset Plan must be submitted to the Minister for approval together with any request for approval under conditions 12(a) and 13(a). 
If any offsets identified in the Offset Plan dated 16 April 2012 are unable to be secured as proposed, the person taking the action must notify the Minister of any changes within 20 business days of the change being known. Any changes to the Offset Plan must be approved by the Minister.
The person taking the action must provide the department with a textual description and map to clearly define the location and boundaries of the direct Offset Area.
