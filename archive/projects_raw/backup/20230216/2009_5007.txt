Water Mouse Offset Strategy
31. The approval holder must develop a Water Mouse Offset Strategy to be submitted for approval by the Minister. The Water Mouse Offset Strategy must:

identify an offset property of at least 80 hectares that will be secured by the approval holder, unless otherwise agreed by the Minister in writing. The offset property must contain habitat that is at least equal habitat quality of the impact site;
the offset property must be located on Curtis Island or within the Port of Gladstone, unless otherwise agreed by the Minister in writing;
outline how the offset property will be secured and the length of the protection mechanism for the property;
demonstrate, with evidence from a suitably qualified expert, that the offset property supports the Water Mouse;
in consultation with a suitably qualified expert, detail how the land will be managed for Water Mouse habitat and population success, including measures for adaptive management;
identify goals and objectives of the offset strategy;
provide details of the offset property including:

maps and site descriptions;
a shapefile containing the exact location of the offset property;
environmental values relevant to Water Mouse including amount of habitat; and
connectivity with other habitats and biodiversity corridors.


contain a research proposal to improve knowledge of the Water Mouse generally and specifically in relation to the population on Curtis Island that is consistent with the Recovery Plan for the Water Mouse. The research proposal must include:

the amount and timing of funds to be provided;
details of who will undertake the research and when;
research outcomes;
endorsement by a suitably qualified expert 


outline how the Water Mouse Offset Strategy facilitates and enables cooperation and collaboration between proponents and other organisations to avoid duplication and maximise environmental benefit.10/12/20133232. The results of research required under condition 31 must be made publically available prior to operation for the life of the approval.10/12/20133333. The approval holder must not commence the action until the Water Mouse Offset Strategy has been approved by the Minister in writing.
