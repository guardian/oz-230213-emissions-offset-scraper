The person taking the action must submit to the Minister, details of arrangements developed to establish and manage a conservation reserve east of Bush Boulevard for the Matted Flax-lily, after the requirements of paragraph 1 have been fulfilled. This plan must include:

A legal instrument that establishes the conservation reserve under a secure form of tenure for conservation purposes only and is attached to the title of the land:
A map delineating the extent of the conservation reserve;
Erection of a fence prior to any construction activities occurring adjacent to the conservation reserve. This fence will allow access to pedestrians, but exclude dogs, bikes and any vehicles that are not required for the maintenance of the conservation reserve;
Funding for fence maintenance, weed and vertebrate pest control, biomass management, monitoring, rubbish removal and other actions necessary to maintain and enhance the Matted Flax-lily population in the conservation reserve for 10 years; and
A copy of a signed agreement between the person taking the action and the person or authority that will manage the conservation reserve outlining arrangements for the management of the site.
