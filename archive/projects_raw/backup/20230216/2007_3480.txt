The report needs to justify the placement and design of these structures.Assessment of the effectiveness of the culverts and underpass for retaining connectivity of habitat for the Southern Brown Bandicoot, which includes preconstruction monitoring for approximately six months and post construction monitoring up to 30 June 2015.
