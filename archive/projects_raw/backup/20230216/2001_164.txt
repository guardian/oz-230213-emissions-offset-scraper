Before carrying out any works in precinct five (except as allowed for in paragraph 7 and paragraph 12), Forester Kurts Properties must establish a population of Allocasuarina emuina at a site agreed in writing by the Minister and must demonstrate to the Minister's satisfaction that:

the population contains at least 850 individuals, and
arrangements are in place to ensure the on-going management of the population for the conservation of Allocasuarina emuina including a suitable fire regime and protection from elevated nutrient levels, and
the population is capable of reproducing and is likely to survive in the long-term.
