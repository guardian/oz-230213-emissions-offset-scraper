The person taking the action must develop a White Box Yellow Box Blakely's Red Gum Grassy Woodland and Derived Native Grassland Management Plan to protect and enhance the Stringybark Hill Reserve (Lot 36 and 37 DP 1101885) Jerrabomberra and Hoover Road offset areas. The Management Plan must specify:

The purpose of the Plan including desired outcomes of implementing the Plan;
The short (within 12 months from the date of this approval), medium (within three years from the date of this approval) and long term (within ten years from the date of this approval) measures that would be employed to implement the Plan;
Details of how the offset areas will be revegetated and rehabilitated over a 10 year period.
Strategies to create and enhance habitat linkages with surrounding woodland remnants;
Details of how the person taking the action will provide for the long term security of the offset areas, including the placement of covenants on the land titles for the offset areas;
Procedures to be implemented for:

Managing the remnant vegetation and habitat on site;
Managing impacts on EPBC listed threatened fauna;
Controlling weeds and feral pests, and
Controlling access.


Performance and completion criteria against which the level of performance and point at which completion of the implementation of the Plan can be determined;
A description of how performance of the implementation of the Plan must be monitored over time against the objectives and completion criteria;
A description of the potential risks to rehabilitation in the offset areas, and a description of the contingency measures that must be implemented to mitigate these risks; and
Details of who is responsible for monitoring, reviewing, implementing and funding the Plan.

The White Box Yellow Box Blakely's Red Gum Grassy Woodland and Derived Native Grassland Management Plan must be submitted to the Minister for approval within 3 months of the date of this approval. The approved Plan must be implemented.
