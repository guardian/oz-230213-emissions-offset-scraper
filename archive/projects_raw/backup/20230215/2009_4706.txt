7. The person taking the action must provide $150 000 (GST exclusive) per annum for 10 years to the WA DEC, as a contribution to trust funds agreed to by the Minister, for the better protection and long term conservation of the EPBC listed fauna species in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. The trust funds, which are managed by WA DEC, will coordinate research that focuses on the following matters amongst others: a) lifecycles; b) threatening processes; c) appropriate land management measures; d) identification of habitat requirements and important populations; and e) long term conservation requirements to ensure persistence of the species in the Pilbara.
Within 3 months of the date of this approval decision the person taking the action must enter into an agreement with the WA DEC for the purposes of the research. The agreement must include:

A commitment for the provision of $70 000 per annum (GST exclusive) for 10 years for research on the EPBC Act listed vulnerable Pilbara Leaf-nosed Bat (Rhinonicteris aurantius);
A commitment for the provision of $50 000 per annum (GST exclusive) for 10 years for research on the EPBC Act listed endangered Northern Quoll (Dasyurus hallucatus);
A commitment for the provision of $30 000 per annum (GST exclusive) for 10 years for research on the EPBC Act listed vulnerable Pilbara Olive Python (Liasis olivaceus barroni)

A copy of the agreement must be provided to the Minister within 10 business days of being signed by both parties.
The first payment must be provided within three months of commencement of construction and subsequent payments made on that date annually.
