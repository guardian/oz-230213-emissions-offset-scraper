To offset impacts on the listed endangered Northern Quoll (Dasyurus hallucatus) the person taking the action must submit a Northern Quoll Research Plan 2020 (NQRP2020) targeted to the Pilbara region of Western Australia, for the approval of the Minister. The plan must include, but is not limited to, the following details:

Methodologies and activities that comprise a research and monitoring program to inform the better protection of the Northern Quoll;
The commitments, including financial, that will be made by the person taking the action to enable the undertaking of the research and monitoring;
The timeframes within which the research and monitoring will be undertaken; and
Timing and methods of reporting the research and monitoring outcomes to the Department, the scientific community and the public.

The NQRP2020 must be accompanied by a statement describing consultation undertaken with the WA DBCA regarding the preparation of the plan, and details of how the NQRP2020 integrates research and monitoring with other Northern Quoll studies implemented and proposed for the Pilbara region.
The person taking the action must submit the NQRP2020 to the Department for approval by  31 November 2020. Until the Minister approves the NQRP2020, the person taking the action must implement the NQRP approved by the Minister.
