To offset the loss of habitat for the Western Ringtail Possum, the person taking the action must prepare and submit, in consultation with WA DEC, a Tuart National Park Rehabilitation Plan (TNPRP) for approval by the Minister. The TNPRP must include:

details of a rehabilitation program for an area of at least 4ha within the Tuart National Park;
aims and objectives of the proposed rehabilitation;
details of weed, pest and fire management;
survival targets proposed for plantings;
contingency measures if survival targets are not achieved;
monitoring and reporting measures;
roles and responsibilities of contractors, staff and the person taking the action; and
timeframes for the implementation and management of the above measures.

The person taking the action must have an approved TNPRP in place within 6 months of the date of the approval.
