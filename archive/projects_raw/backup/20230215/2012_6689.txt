To offset the residual impacts of the action on listed threatened species and ecological communities, the approval holder must provide funding to a conservation offset fund, as approved in writing by the Minister. The approval holder must obtain the approval of the Minister prior to commencement of payments. The funding shall be paid every two years. The first payment will be due on 31 May in the second year following the commencement of the action. The amount of funding to be paid will be calculated and reported as follows:
a. AUD $750 (excluding GST) per hectare of native vegetation cleared within the mine development envelope (delineated in Figure 1 and defined by the geographic coordinates in Schedule 2 of the Western Australian Approval).
b. The real value of contributions described in this condition will be maintained through indexation to the Perth Consumer Price Index (CPI), with the first adjustment to be applied to the first contribution.
c. The approval holder shall prepare and submit an impact reconciliation report for approval of the Minister. The first report will be due on 31 March in the second year following the commencement of the action. The impact reconciliation report shall be submitted every two years on 31 March.
d. The impact reconciliation report shall:
i. require the approval holder to submit spatial data identifying the areas of native vegetation cleared during the previous two years;
