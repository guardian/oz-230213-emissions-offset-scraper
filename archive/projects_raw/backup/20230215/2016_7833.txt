To compensate for the loss of 1.67 ha of CRCIF and Regent Honeyeater habitat (as described in Annexure C and marked as all three shades of green within the red boundary in Annexure B), the approval holder must, prior to the commencement of the action, establish conservation areas on Lots 31 and 32 DP184056 (conservation areas are marked as VMP1 and VMP2 on Annexure A).
The two conservation areas must be a minimum 1.48 ha and 1.49 ha in area respectively and be legally protected by establishing two Positive Covenants under Section 88B of the NSW Conveyancing Act 1919. The covenants must remain in place for the life of the approval, unless replaced as per condition 6.
The approval holder must:
a. notify the Department within 30 days of the Positive Covenants being created,
 
b. ensure that the Positive Covenants include the Minister as a signatory, and
