The person taking the action must provide $100 000 (GST exclusive) per annum for 10 years to the WA DEC,as a contribution to a trust fund agreed to by the department, for the better protection and long term conservation of the EPBC listed Greater Bilby in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. The trust fund will coordinate research on the EPBC listed Greater Bilby (Macrotis lagotis), that focuses the following matters amongst others: a) lifecycles b) threatening processes; c) appropriate land management measures; d) identification of habitat requirements and important populations; and e) long term conservation requirements to ensure persistence of the species.
Within 1 month of commencement of construction the person taking the action must enter into an agreement with the WA DEC for the purposes of Greater Bilby Pilbara population research. The agreement must include:

the aims of the Greater Bilby Pilbara population research;
timeframes for fieldwork; and
timeframes for compilation of fieldwork results.

The first payment must be provided within 1 month of commencement of construction and subsequent payments made on that date annually.
