From the date of commencement of the action the approval holder must provide the following offsets to compensate for residual significant impacts to listed threatened species, as identified in the Middlemount Coal Mine Western Extension Project (EPBC 2017/8130) EPBC Act Preliminary Assessment Documentation March 2019:
a) Brigalow threatened ecological community - 106 ha, comprised of
i. 76 ha of Brigalow and
ii. 30 ha of vegetation in the early stage of regrowing from past clearance.
b) Ornamental Snake habitat - 106 ha, comprised of
i. 76 ha of established Ornamental Snake habitat and
ii. 30 ha of vegetation in the early stage of regrowing from past clearance.
c) Squatter Pigeon habitat - 1, 155 ha, comprised of:
i. 749.5 ha of established known Squatter Pigeon habitat and
ii. 405.5 ha of vegetation in the early stage of regrowing from past clearance.
d) Greater Glider habitat - 975 ha, comprised of:
i. 575 ha of established, known Greater Glider habitat and
ii. 400 ha of vegetation in the early stage of regrowing from past clearance.
e) Koala habitat - 1, 064.5 ha, comprised of:
i. 664.5 ha of established, known Koala habitat and
