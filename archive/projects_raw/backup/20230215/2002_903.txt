Within six months of the date of this approval, and prior to the disturbance of any individual Strzelecki Gum (Eucalyptus strzeleckii) greater than 50 millimetres in diameter at breast height, the person taking the action must submit for the Minister's approval an offset strategy to compensate for impacts on this listed threatened species. The strategy must include:

the establishment of at least 160 individual Strzelecki Gum trees; or measures to ensure the long-term protection and management of at least 160 existing established individual Strzelecki Gum trees, the future protection and survival of which is not currently guaranteed;
measures to ensure the ongoing management of the established Strzelecki Gum trees, including weed control, fire management, erosion and sediment control, and exclusion of livestock;
actions to ensure the long-term protection of the established Strzelecki Gum trees;
the development and implementation of a monitoring program; and
a process to review and report on this offset strategy.
