Prior to making any payment to a conservation offset fund, the approval holder must obtain the approval of the Minister in writing for the amount to be paid and for the conservation offset fund to which it is to be paid.
The payments must be made once every two years. Each payment must be in respect of the area cleared in the 24 months immediately preceding 30 April in the year of payment. The first payment must be paid on or before 30 June in the second calendar year following the commencement of the action.
The amount of each payment must be equivalent, by application of the Perth Consumer Price Index, to the value of AU$3000 (excluding GST) on the date of this approval per hectare of suitable habitat for Northern Quoll, Olive Python (Pilbara subspecies) and Greater Bilby cleared as a result of the approved action.
The approval holder must submit a clearing reconciliation report for approval of the Minister. The first clearing reconciliation report must be submitted between 30 April and 30 May in the second year following the commencement of the action. Each subsequent clearing reconciliation report must be submitted between 30 April and 30 May every second year.
Each clearing reconciliation report must include:


spatial data identifying the areas of suitable habitat for Northern Quoll, Olive Python (Pilbara subspecies) and Greater Bilby cleared during the 24 months immediately preceding 30 April in the reporting year;
