To compensate for the loss of Natural temperate grasslands of the Victorian volcanic plain (NTGVVP), golden sun moth (Synemon plana) habitat and striped legless lizard (Delma impar) habitat, the person taking the action must ensure that a minimum of 25.3 ha of private land in stage 2A, known as the 'tank site' (adjacent to the Western Grasslands Reserve) or the Offset Area, as illustrated in the figure at Attachment A, is protected in perpetuity.13/08/2012022. Within 12 months of the date of this approval, the person taking the action must:

Enter into an agreement under section 173 of the Victorian Planning and Environment Act 198, or equivalent mechanism to achieve the same outcome, to secure the Offset Area for the long-term protection of the NTGVVP, golden sun moth and striped legless lizard population(s) and habitat;
The department must be provided with a copy of the agreement, or equivalent, and evidence of lodgement with the Titles Office within 2 weeks of lodgement;
Ensure that the agreement, or equivalent, is registered on the title on which the Offset Area is located. The department must be provided with evidence of registration within 2 weeks of registration; and
Provide the department with a textual description and map to clearly define the location and boundaries of the Offset Area. This must be accompanied with the offset attributes and a shapefile.13/08/2012033. Within 3 months of the date of this approval, the person taking the action must finalise and submit the Offset Management Plan to the department for the Minister's approval. The Offset Management Plan must provide for the conservation and enhancement of NTGVVP, golden sun moth and striped legless lizard habitat within the Offset Area, and must include:

specific measures to:

control weed species and pest animals;
control access to the protected land;
manage impacts of fire and grazing (acknowledging that such impacts may be positive or negative depending on circumstances);
monitor the status and condition of the NTGVVP, golden sun moth and striped legless lizard population(s) and habitat; and
implement corrective actions and contingency measures where monitoring under the Offset Management Plan indicates a degradation of the NTGVVP, golden sun moth or striped legless lizard habitat.13/08/2012044.
