16. For the better protection and long term conservation of the Greater Bilby, the person taking the action must contribute no less than $400,000 (GST exclusive) to fund, extend or expand a land management program within the Pilbara bioregion. The person taking the action must provide a Greater Bilby Offset Plan (BOP) that includes:

the location of the land area to be managed (mapped and provided to the department in a shapefile or shapefiles);
the on ground management actions which will provide a significant contribution towards the better protection and conservation of the Greater Bilby;
demonstration of how management actions will be undertaken in a manner that is sympathetic with the conservation of EPBC Act listed threatened fauna;
the time frames for undertaking the management actions;
the performance indicators which will determine (in the future) if the management actions required of b) have provided a significant benefit to the Greater Bilby; and
the person(s) responsible for implementation of the plan.

The BOP must be submitted for the approval of the Minister prior to the commencement of the action.
