To compensate for the loss of golden sun moth habitat, spiny rice-flowers and natural temperate grassland of the Victorian volcanic plain, the approval holder must submit to the Department, for approval by the Minister, an Offset Management Plan that is consistent with the EPBC Act environmental offsets policy. The Offset Management Plan must:

include offsets that provide at least:

54.16 hectares of golden sun moth habitat;
54.16 hectares of natural temperate grassland of the Victorian volcanic plain;
230 spiny rice-flowers;


include a site specific spiny rice-flower offset management strategy consistent with the EPBC Act environmental offsets policy; and
