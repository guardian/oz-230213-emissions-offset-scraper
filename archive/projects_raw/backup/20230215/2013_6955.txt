To mitigate impacts to listed threatened species, the person taking the action must, 3 months prior to the intended commencement date of the action, prepare and submit an Environment Management Plan (EMP), outlining measures to protect listed threatened species and improve retained habitat, for the Minister's approval. The EMP must be must be prepared in consultation with DPaW. The person taking the action must not commence the action unless the EMP is approved. The EMP must include, but not be limited to:

Measures during construction to avoid impacts to the listed threatened species on the project site;
Measures to manage access during and after the action to areas specified as "Area A", "Area B" and "Area C" in Attachment 1 and the area specified as "Offset Site" in Attachment 2 to prevent degradation of the habitat in these areas due to rubbish dumping, vehicles, weeds, predators of the Western Ringtail Possum (Pseudocheirus occidentalis) and pests.
Ongoing long-term measures to mitigate impacts to the listed threatened species on the project site and the area specified as "Offset Site" in Attachment 2 after the completion of the action;
Revegetation in the areas specified as "Area B" and "Area C" in Attachment 1 with habitat relevant to listed threatened species including, but not limited to:

Details of the species and amount of seed to be planted;
Details of species to be planted, including number and location;
Objectives, survival targets, timeframes and completion criteria for seeding and planting programs;
Details of contingency measures if targets are not met;


Measures to rehabilitate the area specified as "Offset Site" in Attachment 2;
Details of monitoring and reporting measures, including:

Monitoring targets and trigger values for corrective action;
Methods and frequency of monitoring of and reporting;


Details of tenure and arrangements for ongoing long-term management of the areas specified as "Area A", "Area B" and "Area C" in Attachment 1 and the area specified as "Offset Site" in Attachment 2;
Timeframes for the implementation of the above measures; and
Roles and responsibilities of the personnel associated with implementing each of the above measures.
