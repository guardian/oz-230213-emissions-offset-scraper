The person taking the action must, prior to the commencement of construction, prepare and submit an Environmental Offsets Strategy for the Ministers approval to offset the impacts on the Mahogany Glider. The Strategy must identify offsets that:

Offset the loss of Mahogany Glider habitat;
Re-establish links between fragmented Mahogany Glider habitat; and .
Increase knowledge and understanding of the impacts of habitat loss and fragmentation on the Mahogany Glider and the measures to reduce such impacts.

The person taking the action must not commence construction of the transmission line unless the Minister has approved the Environmental Offset Strategy, with the exception of the area identified at Annexure B.
