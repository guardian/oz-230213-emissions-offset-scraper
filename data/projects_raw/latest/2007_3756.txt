The person taking the action must require the proposed lessee of up to 38.83ha of Commonwealth land at the Majura Training Area site to enter into a conservation agreement with the Minister for the Environment, Heritage and the Arts to protect and conserve the biodiversity of the area; in particular the ecological community Natural Temperate Grassland and listed threatened species, prior to concluding the lease agreement.12/04/200822. The conservation agreement outlined in paragraph 1 must require the lessee of the Commonwealth land to retain and manage the site for Natural Temperate Grassland and listed threatened species.
The conservation agreement must address the following matters:

maintain maximum connectivity between eastern sections of Natural Temperate Grassland;
minimise fragmentation and edge effects on Natural Temperate Grassland by maximising the edge to area ratio;
provide for the conservation in perpetuity of areas of high quality Natural Temperate Grassland;
exclude development on the site, except for a road with a maximum total easement width of 3 lanes;
construct the road as close as possible to the airport perimeter fence, taking account of safety requirements;
lighting along the road must address impacts on listed threatened species;
exclude signage (except road safety signs) along the road; and
