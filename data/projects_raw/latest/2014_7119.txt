The approval holder must submit within three (3) months of the approval of this action, to the Department, for the Minister’s approval, a BioBank Site Management Plan, for the conservation and management of MNES present within the BioBank Sites. The BioBank Site Management Plan must be prepared by a suitably qualified person and must include:

details of management actions to be undertaken to protect, conserve and manage GHFF habitat, CPW and SSTF within the BioBank Sites;
clear objectives and performance indicators for all management actions;

details of corrective actions should trigger levels be exceeded, measures to manage weeds;
measures to prevent the occurrence of dieback by Phytophthora cinnamomi during construction; and
a commitment to manage the BioBank Sites for the life of the impacts and the persons responsible for management actions.
