The person taking the action must prepare and submit an Environmental Management Plan (EMP) for the Minister's approval to mitigate and offset impacts to the Dwarf Galaxias. The person taking the action must not commence the action unless the Minister has approved the EMP. The EMP must include, but is not limited to, the following information:

sedimentation, erosion and pollution controls, including an emergency response procedure to address any unforeseen discharge or pollutants into Boggy Creek;
details on construction and maintenance including fencing, stockpiling of chemical and fuels and contractor induction;
details on weed management;
details on the revegetation of the Boggy Creek riparian zone;
specific details, maps and diagrams on the construction of off-stream wetlands including proposed construction activities within the 5 metre buffer, as specified in condition 2 c);
details on how the offset measures specified in d) and e) of condition 3 meet the departments Offset Policy; and
details of a regular monitoring program including measures to effectively maintain and/or improve both the water quality of Boggy Creek and the presence of Dwarf Galaxias in Boggy Creek. The program must also monitor and report on revegetation activities in the Boggy Creek riparian zone.
