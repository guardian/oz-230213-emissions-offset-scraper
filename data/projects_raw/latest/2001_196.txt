The Department of Infrastructure must prepare and submit to the Minister for approval prior to commencement of construction of the bridge across Yuroke Creek, a plan for managing the impacts of the construction of the bridge across Yuroke Creek on the Southern Bell Frog (Litoria Raniformis) including:

where bridge pylons will be located to minimise disturbance to habitat;
drainage channels and sedimentation ponds to prevent sediment runoff into Yuroke Creek;
how shading and noise impacts will be minimised, including details of the design of the bridge;
a programme of monitoring of sediment runoff during and after construction; and 
identification and creation or enhancement of habitat to offset degradation of habitat.
