The German Creek East Joint Venture (Capcoal) must prepare and submit to the Minister for approval a strategy to manage the impacts of mining operations on listed Commonwealth threatened species and communities, and the management of conservation of areas. The strategy must address management of the following areas:

Conservation Areas A and B, that are subject to Conservation Covenants (ANNEXURE 3) between the company and the Queensland Environment Protection Authority, as marked in ANNEXURE 2;
The corridor abutting Conservation Area A, as marked in ANNEXURE 2; and 
Other populations of the Brigalow ecological community (Acacia harpophylla dominant and co-dominant) within the mine lease.

The strategy must be provided to the Minister within 6 months of the approval of the action.
