To offset the loss of habitat for Carnaby's Black-Cockatoo, the person taking the action must, within 12 months of the commencement of construction, provide funds to WA DEC for the acquisition, and a contribution to management of the offset described in Alkimos Lot 1004 Residential Development Mitigation and Offsets Strategy for Matters of National Environmental Significance (Eco Logical Australia, January 2012).
