The management plan must contain measures to address the following matters on Block 17 Section 102 Symonston (see map at Annexure 1). The management plan must also have a commitment that the lease agreement for the relevant block must also address the relevant matters below:

Prevent any encroachment on Grassland Earless Dragon habitat within the adjacent rural lease during construction on Block 17;
Design and construct the drainage system on Block 17 to divert runoff away from the adjacent rural lease;
Ensure that major structures and trees on Block 17 are only located on the site where they would not shade, in a way that would damage, the Grassland Earless Dragon habitat on the adjacent rural lease;
Implement bushfire protection measures in a way which maintains or enhances the quality of Grassland Earless Dragon habitat outside the site boundary on Block 18;
Prevent weeds from spreading from Block 17 site into neighbouring native pasture;
Ensure that people on Block 17 are not provided with access to the adjacent rural lease;
Prevent domestic pets from entering the adjacent rural lease from Block 17;
Limit the opportunity for predatory birds to perch on fences and trees within Block 17, in proximity to Grassland Earless Dragon habitat on the adjacent rural lease;
Maintain or enhance the quality of remaining Grassland Earless Dragon habitat in the adjacent rural lease (Block 18); and
Maintain or enhance the quality of Grassland Earless Dragon habitat throughout the Jerrabomberra Valley beyond Block 17.

Construction must not commence until the management plan has been approved by the Minister.
