Within 12 months of the date of this approval, the person taking the action must submit for the Minister's approval a plan to offset the loss of 6.4 ha of Semi Evergreen Vine Thicket ecological community from the project area and to manage this community. The plan must include:

a map detailing the location of the offset, its associated vegetation types, habitat values and infrastructure;
measures for the long-term protection of at least an equivalent existing area(s) of remnant or regrowth Semi Evergreen Vine Thicket ecological community;
measures to survey and monitor the occurrence of flora and fauna species including, but not limited to, Star Finch (Neochmia ruficauda), Black-breasted Button Quail (Turnix melanogaster), Eastern Long-eared Bat (Nyctophilus timoriensis), Yakka Skink (Egernia rugosa), Brigalow Scaly-foot (Paradelma orientalis), Quassia bidwillii, Cossinia australiana, Trymalium minutiflorum and Cycas megacarpa;
measures for weed and feral animal control, fire management, erosion and sediment control, exclusion of livestock, and restrictions on access;
the development and implementation of a monitoring program including performance criteria against which the effectiveness of this plan can be measured; and
a process to review and report on this plan and on any unplanned events which may impact the offset.

Coal mining operations must not commence until the plan has been approved.
