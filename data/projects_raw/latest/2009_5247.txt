To compensate for impacts on the Spiny Rice-flower the person taking the action must, within 12 months of the date of this approval, secure the approval by the Minister of a suitable biodiversity offset. The protected land must:

include an existing population of at least 3 Spiny Rice-flower plants;
provide for additional conservation benefits, such as landscape connectivity, resilience and long-term viability, proximity to impact area and existing reserves, protection of critical or priority habitat areas, and protection of additional threatened species or their habitat; and
be planted with an additional 28 Spiny Rice-flower plants within 18 months of the date of this approval and include measures to ensure survival of the additional Spiny Rice-flower plants within the Offset Management Plan referred to in condition 6.1/12/2012033. To establish the biodiversity offset the person taking the action must, prior to the commencement of construction, enter into a written agreement with Trust for Nature ("the Engagement Agreement") to identify potential sites to form the basis of the biodiversity offset (as required under Condition 2) and to secure the protection in perpetuity of the protected land. A copy of the Engagement Agreement must be provided to the department within 7 days of its signature by all parties.7/02/2012044. To establish the biodiversity offset the person taking the action must, prior to the commencement of construction, provide a sum of $500,000 (five hundred thousand dollars) to Trust for Nature to be held until such time as the biodiversity offset is approved by the Minister, at which time the money will form the basis for the purchase of environmental services from the owner of the biodiversity offset. If the cost of purchasing the environmental services is greater than $500,000 the person taking the action must provide additional funds to Trust for Nature as required. If the cost of purchasing the environmental services is less than $500,000 surplus funds will be returned to the person taking the action.
The arrangements for the provision of expenditure of the funds must be set out in an agreement ("the Funding Agreement"). A draft copy of the Funding Agreement must be provided to the department for approval prior to its signing by all parties.
