To offset the loss of 3.1 ha of Western Ringtail Possum habitat, the person taking the action must, within six (6) months of the commencement of construction, commence rehabilitation of 3.4 ha of degraded land identified as 'Revegetation Area' at Attachment B, in accordance with the Environmental Offsets Management Plan detailed at Condition 4.13/12/2012044. To offset impacts to the Western Ringtail Possum, the person taking the action must prepare and submit an Environmental Offsets Management Plan (EOMP). The EOMP must include:

details of the proposed offset site including its suitability for offsetting impacts to the Western Ringtail Possum as a result of this action;
measures to ensure the future long term protection of the offset site;
details of the rehabilitation actions proposed to take place within the 3.4 ha of degraded land within the offset site;
measures to exclude people, pests, weeds and Dieback (Phytopthora cinnamomi) from the offset site;
maps of the proposed offset site showing vegetation quality, possum sightings, drey locations, areas being rehabilitated and dieback free zones;
performance indicators for rehabilitation and Western Ringtail Possum protection;
details of monitoring, reporting and contingency measures if performance indicators are not met;
timeframes for the implementation and completion of the above measures; and,
roles and responsibilities of personnel associated with implementing each of the above measures.

The EOMP must be submitted to the Department prior to the commencement of construction. If the Minister approves the EOMP, the approved EOMP must be implemented. Construction must not commence until the EOMP is approved by the Minister.13/12/2012066.
