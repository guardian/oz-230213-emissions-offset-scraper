To offset the loss of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and potential Golden Sun Moth habitat, the approval holder must secure, protect and manage no less than 3.40 hectares of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and 3.86 hectares of Golden Sun Moth habitat. The nominated property is located at 833 Pura Road, Darlington, Victoria, (the Terrinalum South Offset) as specified in the offset management plan. The Terrinalum South Offset must be protected pursuant to a statutory agreement made under section 69 of the Conservation Forests and Lands Act 1987 (Vic) or section 173 of the Planning and Environment Act 1987 (Vic). The statutory agreement must contain covenants protecting the Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and Golden Sun Moth within the Terrinalum South Offset against inconsistent use and development, and must be registered on title. Evidence of lodgement of the agreement with the Registrar of Titles must be provided to the Department within 90 days of the commencement of the action or within the period agreed to in writing by the Minister.18/02/201502condition attached to the approval
