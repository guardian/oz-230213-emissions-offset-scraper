The approval holder must provide for the implementation of a suite of offset measures to minimise on and off-Site impacts of the development on the Southern Cassowary, to the written satisfaction of the Department. The offset measures are to include:

The establishment of a Conservation Agreement under s.305 of the Environment Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act (EPBC) 1999, between the Department and the approval holder. The Conservation Agreement is to be over 55ha of land, as agreed by the Department as appropriate Southern Cassowary habitat, and is to include the provision for funding for initial and ongoing weed management and fencing activities;
Funding of Southern Cassowary habitat vegetation restoration activities and weed and pest control over 0.5ha on Lot 98 (SP175431), to the immediate North of the Site; and
Funding of Southern Cassowary habitat vegetation restoration activities and weed and pest control over 4.8ha of appropriate bushland to enhance biodiversity values.
