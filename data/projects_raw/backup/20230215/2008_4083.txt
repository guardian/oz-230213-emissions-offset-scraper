Each covenant must provide for the protection of these Conservation areas in perpetuity by ensuring that the land shall only be used for nature conservation purposes, and preventing future development activities occurring in such areas which are inconsistent with that purpose.
Each covenant must specify that the Minister's prior approval in writing is required in order to alter or remove the covenants on any of the Conservation areas defined in Annexure A and Annexure B.
Works must not commence until the Minister has provided written notification to the person taking the action that the Minister is satisfied the covenant will protect these areas in perpetuity.21/02/2011055.
