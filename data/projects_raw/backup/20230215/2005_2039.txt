Prior to the operation of the out-of-pit spoil dumps, the person taking the action must submit for the Minister's approval, a plan for managing the area of Bluegrass (Dichanthium spp.) dominant grasslands ecological community identified in ANNEXURE 3. The plan must include:

a process for providing long-term protection to the area of Bluegrass (Dichanthium spp.) dominant grasslands ecological community;
measures for clearly defining the on-ground boundaries of this area;
a process for monitoring grassland condition, species diversity, ground cover, and weed occurrence;
the management of weed species; fire, including emergency response in the event of wild fires, and grazing; and
a process for reporting on and reviewing the effectiveness of this plan.

Operation of the out-of-pit spoil dumps must not commence until the plan has been approved.
