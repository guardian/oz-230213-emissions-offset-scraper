The proponent must prepare a roadside vegetation offset management plan for the Princes Highway between Sheepwash Creek and Flynn. This plan must be approved by the department prior to commencement of the action. The approved plan must be implemented. At a minimum the roadside vegetation offset management plan must:

include commitments for offsetting the impact to the critically endangered ecological community Gippsland Red Gum (Eucalyptus tereticornis subsp. mediana) Grassy Woodland and Associated Native Grassland;
include specific details, including maps and diagrams that show boundaries of the proposed offset site;
include information and commitments about management arrangements that will be employed, now and into the future to ensure the ongoing rehabilitation and improvement of the offset site;
demonstrate how the offset site will be protected in perpetuity;
include information and commitments to monitoring and reporting on the progress of the offset site;
