The Offset Management Plan must provide for the conservation and enhancement of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and Golden Sun Moth populations and habitat within the Saxon Paddock Offset, and must include:

specific measures to:

control weed species and pest animals;
control access to the protected land;
manage impacts of fire and grazing (acknowledging that such impacts may be positive or negative depending on circumstances);
monitor the status and condition of the Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and Golden Sun Moth population and habitat; and
report the results of monitoring and management activities to the department;


measures to ensure that actions taken to conserve and enhance the Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and Golden Sun Moth population and habitat have no detrimental impact on the populations or habitat of other listed threatened species and communities that are identified as occurring on the protected land;
corrective actions and contingency measures to be implemented where monitoring under the Offset Management Plan indicates a degradation of the Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain or Golden Sun Moth population or habitat; and
