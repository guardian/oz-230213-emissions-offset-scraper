The EMP must be developed in consultation with Parks Australia North and the Kakadu National Park Board of Management and include measures to:
a)	address weed management prior to and during construction to ensure that activities associated with the action do not:
i.	assist the spread of weeds within Kakadu National Park;
ii.	introduce weeds into Kakadu National Park; or
iii.	increase the abundance of weeds in infested areas within Kakadu National Park.
b)	provide an offset to Parks Australia North to assist implementation of weed management measures within Kakadu National Park and to monitor and manage the route post construction;
c)	ensure that revegetation along the route occurs at a rate and standard acceptable to Parks Australia North;
d)	reduce the nominal clearing width at the Magela Creek crossing to a maximum of 4m;
e)	must avoid impacts on riparian vegetation either side of East Alligator River; 
f)	identify and mark ground nests,  trees with hollows or nests and mature trees along the route before construction and ensure that these are avoided during construction;
g)	rescue or relocate any disturbed or injured fauna;
h)	ensure open trenches do not pose a threat to fauna;
i)	mitigate and remediate soil erosion and sedimentation; 
j)	ensure that recognised Traditional Owner/s are present onsite during the clearing and ripping stages of construction to identify, monitor and advise on effects to country, sacred sites and archaeological areas;
k)	realign the route to avoid any identified sacred sites and archaeological areas, including any identified by the Traditional Owner/s during construction;
Construction must not commence until the plan has been approved. The approved plan must be implemented.
