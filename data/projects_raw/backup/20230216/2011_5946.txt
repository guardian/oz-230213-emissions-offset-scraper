To compensate for the loss of 5.87 hectares of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain, the person taking the action must secure a biodiversity offset located at 435 McDonnells Road, Birregurra. The biodiversity offset must include the protection in perpetuity of at least 23.5 hectares of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain ecological community and must be secured by an appropriate covenant prior to the commencement of construction activities.25/04/2013044. The person taking the action must undertake the ongoing management of the biodiversity offset in accordance with the Jacksons Hill Offset Management Plan. The person taking the action must notify the department of all proposed changes and revisions to the plan. Depending on the nature and extent of changes, the department may request the revised plan be submitted for re-approval. The Jacksons Hill Offset Management Plan must be implemented.25/04/2013055. Prior to the commencement of construction activities, the person taking the action must provide the department with illustrations and maps that clearly define the location and boundaries of the offset site. This must be accompanied with the offset attributes and a shapefile for each offset site.25/04/2013066. The person taking the action must monitor the condition of the biodiversity offset as outlined in the Jacksons Hill Offset Management Plan.
