To compensate for the loss of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and habitat for the Golden Sun Moth the person taking the action must provide a biodiversity offset package to protect a minimum of 14.08 hectares each of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and habitat for the Golden Sun Moth.19/08/2013033. At least three months prior to the commencement of construction, the person taking the action must submit to the Minister for approval a biodiversity offset package that must address the following:

ensure that at least 12.35 hectares of land corresponding to Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain and that provides habitat for the Golden Sun Moth, including confirmed presence of Golden Sun Moth, of equal or higher quality to that identified in Condition 1, is protected and managed in perpetuity (known as the Offset Area); and
ensure that Offset Zone 1 is secured for perpetuity and managed for a minimum of ten years for the protection of Natural Temperate Grassland of the Victorian Volcanic Plain, habitat for the Golden Sun Moth and five known Spiny Rice-flower plants.

Construction must not commence until the biodiversity offset package has been approved by the Minister. The biodiversity offset package must be implemented as approved.19/08/2013044.
