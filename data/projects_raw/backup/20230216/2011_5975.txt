No. . sort ascendingDetails. sort descendingStart Date. sort descending1111. Contribution to Research for Threatened Fauna Species
The person taking the action must provide a financial contribution of $400,000 (GST exclusive) for research that will contribute to the better protection and long term conservation of EPBC Act listed threatened fauna species in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. The research must contain, but is not limited to; identifying the key threat management actions required to restore and maintain functioning populations of EPBC Act listed threatened species in the Pilbara, and understanding actions that are required to protect listed threatened species and the habitat they utilise, taking into account the costs and feasibility.

the person taking the action must enter into an agreement with a research institution to provide the financial contribution. The agreement must include clear timeframes in which financial contributions will be paid. The person taking the action cannot continue construction or operation beyond 31 May 2012 unless the agreement has been formalised. Unless approved by the Minister, the person taking the action cannot continue construction or operation of the action if the milestones agreed to by the proponent in the funding agreement are not achieved and the final payment is not made within 30 days of the completion of the research.
A copy of the agreement must be provided to the Minister within 10 business days of the agreement being finalised. The person proposing to take the action must implement the agreement.
