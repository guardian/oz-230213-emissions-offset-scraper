The person taking the action must submit a Biodiversity Offset Strategy for the three listed threatened ecological communities listed at condition 1 to the Minister for approval. The strategy must address the following requirements:

The re-establishment of no less than 1020 ha of vegetation identified as the EPBC Act listed Natural Grasslands of the Queensland Central Highlands and Northern Fitzroy Basin ecological community;
The re-establishment of no less than 95 ha of vegetation identified as the EPBC Act listed Semi-Evergreen Vine Thickets of the Brigalow Belt (North and South) and Nandewar Bioregions ecological community;
The re-establishment of no less than 20 ha of vegetation identified as the EPBC Act listed Brigalow (Acacia harpophylla dominant and co-dominant) ecological community;
The areas referred to in conditions 2 (a), (b) and (c) must be located within 100 km of the Rolleston Coal operation unless otherwise agreed to by the Minister;
The strategy must detail how the areas referred to in conditions 2 (a), (b) and (c) will contribute to habitat connectivity between existing conservation reserves in the region;
The strategy must detail how the areas referred to in conditions 2 (a), (b) and (c) will be protected by a legal instrument under relevant nature conservation legislation, to ensure that the land is conserved in perpetuity; and
The strategy must detail timeframes for the completion of all actions outlined in the strategy, including mechanisms for reporting on progress toward successful re-establishment of the areas referred to in conditions 2 (a), (b) and (c).

The person taking the action must not clear any vegetation listed in Conditions 1a) and 1c) until the Biodiversity Offset Strategy is approved by the Minister. The strategy must be submitted to the Minister within 18 months of the date of this approval.
